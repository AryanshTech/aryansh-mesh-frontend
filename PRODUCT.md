# Aryansh Mesh — Product Summary

Unified CRM frontend: agency marketing (primary) and SMB business ops in one shell, plus platform admin for tenants.

| Area | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Business Manager | Products, clients, bookings, costs, content, publish, website connect |
| Marketing Hub | Projects, threads, brand memory, social calendar, creative workflows |
| Admin | Platform-level tenant management |

See [README.md](README.md) for setup and [docs/ARCHITECTURE.md](docs/ARCHITECTURE.md) for module boundaries, auth, and data flow.
